Archaeology Fun Facts!

Many sites are still mysteries yet to be explained, such as Stonehenge.
Archaeologists actually are not the ones digging for dinosaurs (those are paleontologists)! They are searching for facts about human history and how we have changed over time, along with what life was like back then.
The first ever archaeologist is actually considered to be a king from the Neo-Babylonian Empire, called Nabonidus!
