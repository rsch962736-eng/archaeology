Famous archaeological sites

The Great Wall of China - A wall that spans over more than 13,000 miles, the Great Wall of China was built to defend China's northern border.

Machu Picchu - Often referred to as "Lost City of the Incas", Machu Picchu was likely built as a royal estate, with religious and astronomical significance.

Acropolis of Athens - Build on an outcrop in Athens, Greece, its purpose was to honor Athena, the greek goddess, defend Athens, and to celebrate their victory over the Persians.
